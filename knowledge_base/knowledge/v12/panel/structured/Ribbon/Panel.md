## Default Properties

Here you modify the default settings of panels.

The settings you have made will be applied to all future panels.

You can reset your settings to the standard settings.

## Resize

When the grid is activated, the panel size can be changed only as a multiple of the grid width or grid height, regardless of whether you change the size using the selecting points or the Panel ribbontab.
